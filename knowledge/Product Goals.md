# Purpose

Describe the outcomes Arcana aims to achieve for its users.

---

# Goals

## Help users make better investment decisions

Support confident decision-making by presenting evidence,
context and meaningful insights.

---

## Simplify complex financial analysis

Transform large volumes of portfolio and market data into
information that can be explored efficiently.

---

## Increase confidence through transparency

Help users understand why insights are generated rather than
simply presenting recommendations.

---

## Enable comparative analysis

Support meaningful comparison between portfolios,
securities, benchmarks and scenarios.

---

## Reduce analytical effort

Help users reach conclusions faster without sacrificing
understanding.

---

# Design Implications

- Prioritize understanding over speed.
- Preserve context across workflows.
- Encourage evidence-based exploration.
- Support comparison naturally.

---

# AI Interpretation

When multiple interface solutions are possible,
prefer the one that better supports the product goals.

Never optimize purely for visual simplicity if it
reduces analytical understanding.
