# Purpose

Scenario analysis enables users to evaluate how portfolios might behave under hypothetical market conditions.

The objective is to understand potential future outcomes before making investment decisions.

---

# Why It Matters

Users use scenario analysis to:

- test assumptions
- evaluate resilience
- compare alternative strategies
- understand tradeoffs before acting

---

# Design Implications

Interfaces should:

- encourage comparison between scenarios
- preserve the baseline context
- clearly distinguish projected outcomes from historical data
- communicate uncertainty appropriately

---

# AI Interpretation

Scenario analysis is exploratory rather than definitive.

Generated interfaces should emphasize comparison, assumptions and potential outcomes rather than certainty.
