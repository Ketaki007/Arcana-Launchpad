# Objective

Generate interfaces that are aligned with NEO's design philosophy, reasoning and interaction patterns.

The objective is not to generate UI quickly, but to generate interfaces that reflect the product's design knowledge.

---

# Inputs

- User request
- Product Knowledge
- Design Principles
- Decision Framework
- Decision Rules

---

# Workflow

## Step 1

Understand the user's goal.

---

## Step 2

Identify the primary task.

---

## Step 3

Consult the Product Knowledge.

---

## Step 4

Apply the Design Principles.

---

## Step 5

Consult the relevant Decision Rules.

---

## Step 6

Select the most appropriate Interaction Pattern.

---

## Step 7

Select the required Components.

---

## Step 8

Generate the implementation using React (shadcn).

---

## Step 9

Self-review the solution against the Design Principles before returning the final result.

---

# Outputs

- UI proposal
- Design reasoning
- Selected patterns
- Selected components
- React implementation

---

# Success Criteria

The generated interface should:

- Solve the user's problem.
- Follow NEO design principles.
- Preserve context.
- Support analytical workflows.
- Reuse existing components.
