# Objective

Review interfaces for consistency with NEO's product philosophy, design principles and interaction patterns.

The agent should explain its reasoning rather than simply identifying issues.

---

# Inputs

- Design mockup or UI
- Product Knowledge
- Design Principles
- Decision Rules
- Interaction Patterns

---

# Workflow

## Step 1

Identify the user's primary goal.

---

## Step 2

Identify the interaction patterns being used.

---

## Step 3

Evaluate the design against the Design Principles.

---

## Step 4

Validate important design decisions using the Decision Rules.

---

## Step 5

Identify inconsistencies, usability issues and missed opportunities.

---

## Step 6

Provide actionable recommendations with supporting reasoning.

---

# Outputs

- Design strengths
- Design issues
- Recommendations
- Knowledge references
- Overall assessment

---

# Success Criteria

The review should:

- Be objective.
- Reference the knowledge system.
- Explain every recommendation.
- Prioritize high-impact improvements.
- Maintain consistency with NEO.
