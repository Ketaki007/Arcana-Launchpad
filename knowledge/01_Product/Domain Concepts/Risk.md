# Purpose

Risk represents the uncertainty associated with investment outcomes.

Understanding risk is as important as understanding returns.

---

# Why It Matters

Users evaluate both opportunity and uncertainty before making investment decisions.

Risk is not an exception state—it is a continuous dimension of portfolio analysis.

---

# Design Implications

Interfaces should:

- expose risk alongside performance
- highlight changes in risk over time
- explain sources of increased risk
- avoid presenting risk as an isolated metric

---

# AI Interpretation

Never present performance without considering associated risk.

Where appropriate, connect risk to contributing factors rather than displaying standalone values.
