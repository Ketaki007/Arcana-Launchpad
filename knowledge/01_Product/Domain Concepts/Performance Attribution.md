# Purpose

Performance attribution explains why a portfolio generated its observed returns.

Rather than reporting performance alone, attribution identifies the underlying contributors.

---

# Why It Matters

Investment professionals seek explanations, not just results.

Understanding which sectors, securities or factors contributed to performance enables better future decisions.

---

# Design Implications

Interfaces should:

- reveal contributing factors
- support drill-down exploration
- prioritize explanation over summary metrics
- preserve traceability between results and evidence

---

# AI Interpretation

Whenever performance is displayed, consider whether users also need an explanation of the underlying drivers.

Prefer interfaces that expose relationships rather than isolated values.
