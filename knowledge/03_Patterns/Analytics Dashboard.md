# Purpose

Present a high-level overview of portfolio performance while enabling users to progressively explore supporting information.

---

# Structure

- Summary KPIs
- Key visualizations
- Important alerts or insights
- Detailed analytical views
- Supporting tables

---

# Design Guidelines

- Prioritize information hierarchy.
- Surface important changes immediately.
- Support drill-down exploration.
- Preserve active filters and context.

---

# AI Interpretation

Dashboards should answer:

- What happened?
- Why did it happen?
- Where should users investigate next?

Avoid overwhelming users with excessive detail on the initial view.
