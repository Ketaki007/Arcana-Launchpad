# Purpose

Enable users to enter or modify information efficiently while maintaining context.

---

# Design Guidelines

- Group related inputs.
- Validate early.
- Minimize required fields.
- Preserve progress.
- Provide clear feedback.

---

# AI Interpretation

Forms should reduce cognitive load and support successful completion with minimal interruption.
