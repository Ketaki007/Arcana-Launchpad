# Industry Context

## Why This Document Exists

Arcana Launchpad is designed for professional financial users operating in institutional environments. This document provides the domain knowledge required to make informed product and design decisions.

## Understanding the Domain

Professional financial work revolves around continuous decision-making rather than sequential task completion. Users spend their day monitoring markets, comparing information, identifying patterns, and making high-value decisions under time pressure.

The workspace itself becomes the product.

## Characteristics of Financial Work

### Information-Dense
Professionals require simultaneous access to multiple sources of information.

### Continuous Rather Than Sequential
Analysis is exploratory and non-linear.

### Context Is Critical
The value comes from relationships between widgets rather than individual screens.

### Time Sensitive
Understanding what changed is often more valuable than finding information.

## Industry Interaction Patterns

- Modular workspaces
- Widget-based architecture
- Shared context
- Persistent workspaces
- Keyboard-oriented interaction

## Where Existing Products Create Friction

- Workspace setup
- Configuration complexity
- Context restoration
- Knowledge fragmentation

## Arcana's Perspective

Arcana Launchpad preserves proven interaction models while modernizing them through Interaction Enhancements and Intelligent Layers.

## Design Implications

Design should preserve mental models, respect information density, reduce interaction cost, strengthen context, and keep users in control.
