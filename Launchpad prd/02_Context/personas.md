# Professional Workflow Profiles

## Why This Document Exists

Arcana Launchpad is designed around professional workflows rather than demographic personas. These profiles describe how different users think, work and interact with the platform.

---

# Equity Analyst

## Primary Objective

Research companies and produce investment recommendations.

## Typical Activities

- Review company fundamentals
- Analyze financial statements
- Compare competitors
- Monitor earnings and news
- Document findings

## Typical Workspace

Research View
- Company Overview
- Financial Analysis
- Relative Valuation
- News
- Chart

## Success Criteria

- Reach evidence-backed conclusions efficiently.
- Maintain continuity across long investigations.

## Pain Points

- Repetitive workspace setup
- Fragmented information
- Context loss after interruptions

---

# Portfolio Manager

## Primary Objective

Monitor portfolio performance and make allocation decisions.

## Typical Activities

- Track holdings
- Monitor market events
- Compare portfolio positions
- Review risk exposure

## Typical Workspace

Portfolio View
- Watchlist
- Portfolio Holdings
- Chart
- News

## Success Criteria

- Understand portfolio health quickly.
- Detect changes that require action.

## Pain Points

- Monitoring multiple assets simultaneously
- Maintaining situational awareness

---

# Trader

## Primary Objective

React quickly to changing market conditions.

## Typical Activities

- Monitor live markets
- Track price movement
- Review breaking news
- Execute rapid decisions

## Typical Workspace

Market View
- Watchlist
- Chart
- News
- Security Overview

## Success Criteria

- Minimize reaction time.
- Access critical information immediately.

## Pain Points

- Information overload
- Constant context switching
